Commercial Floor Cleaning And Renovating

Metro Denver - Commercial Floor & Carpet Cleaning

Maintaining a clean floor is essential for any medical center, business owner or commercial space manager. We offer commercial floor cleaning packages that will keep your space looking it's best, with services scheduled to fit your schedule and your budget.

Your Floors are a BIG Investment!

Do the people you have cleaning your floors understand the importance of your floors to your business? Or how much it’ll cost you to replace flooring that is not taken care of properly? WE DO!! At High Plains Services we understand the value of that “HIGH SHINE APPEARANCE” and how this reflects on your business. We also understand the high cost of floor replacement! Unnecessary stripping can damage your floors and make replacement necessary much sooner, costing your business unneeded expense. Your flooring says a lot about the way you run your business. A clean, well-maintained office or storefront can entice clients and encourage productivity. But dirty, dingy floors can make a negative impression on employees, patients and potential customers alike.

The environment you work in determines much of how you feel about the place you work. Everyone needs a nice, clean, bright place to be productive. But even more important are the customers that judge your business by the cleanliness they see when they walk in the door. They expect a bright environment. We give you that brightness.

Our employees are trained to look for problems. If they arrive for a service and determine something is wrong in the store, they immediately notify our management. Management determines whether it's something we need to wake one of your managers for, something we can take care of and leave you a note, or if it's not important enough to take action on, our crew will leave you a note with what they found. Your business is your livelihood. When you hire our services we understand that the effectiveness of our work is linked closely to presenting your business in a professional light. That’s why we take our customer’s best interests as our best interests. Every one of our employees feels this way. If they didn’t, they wouldn’t work for us. We strive to do our best to make sure your stores are in the best shape possible, and are ready to open by the time your crew arrives in the morning.

We are more than just another floor service. We consider ourselves an important part of keeping your business looking great and operating properly.

We have almost 20 years of experience in commercial floor and carpet maintenance, with many long term happy customers. References provided upon request.

Denver Metro – Floor Renovation Service

Renovating your Denver Metro business can be even more stressful than moving. Anyone who has ever made additions or changes to their business knows how chaotic it can sometimes be. High Plains Services is experienced in the process of cleaning floors at construction and renovation sites during and after the work is completed. With a keen focus on detail, we will make sure your new floors are exactly what you’re looking for.

High Plains Services will also work with you to set up a regular service to keep your new floor in tip top shape and looking good. With our trusted staff and excellent service, you’ll have one less thing to worry about so you can get back running your business as soon as possible!
